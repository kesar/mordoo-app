## Value Proposition
- **Problem:** Spiritually curious people in Thailand lack a modern, affordable, personalized astrology app rooted in authentic Thai traditions. Existing options are either expensive Western apps (฿599+/mo) with no Thai cultural relevance, or basic Thai fortune-telling apps with no AI personalization.
- **Target Audience:** Spiritually curious Thai adults (25-45), primarily female, who check horoscopes/fortune daily. Secondary: English-speaking expats in Thailand and Thai diaspora seeking cultural connection. Thailand is 65%+ Android — must be first-class on both platforms.
- **Unique Differentiator:** The only app that blends authentic Thai Siam Si (28 fortune sticks) with Western numerology and AI-powered personalized guidance (Claude) — all bilingual, at 1/4 the price of Western competitors.
- **Elevator Pitch:** Mor Doo is your AI-powered Thai fortune guide — blending Siam Si tradition, numerology, and personalized cosmic wisdom in Thai and English for ฿149/month.
