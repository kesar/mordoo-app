| Secondary accent | #f4e8c1 (parchment, for highlights) |
| Format | PNG or JPEG, no transparency |

### Style Guidelines

- **Mood:** Mystical, celestial, premium, spiritual
- **Core motifs:** Constellation lines, gold orbs, star particles, Thai yantra patterns, lotus, dharma wheel
- **Typography on card:** Optional — a single short tagline in CinzelDecorative or CormorantGaramond is acceptable but not required. Thai text should use NotoSansThai.
- **Do NOT include:** Mordoo logo (it already appears in the event listing), pricing, cluttered text
- **Contrast:** Ensure gold elements are visible against the dark background at thumbnail scale
- **Safe zone:** Keep key visual elements within the center 80% — edges may be cropped on some devices

## 6. Best Practices for Mordoo

### Frequency & Timing

- **Minimum:** 1 event per month to maintain algorithmic visibility on the Today tab
- **Ideal:** 2 events per month (1 cultural + 1 feature/challenge)
- **Maximum:** 10 events live/scheduled at once (Apple limit) — never exceed 5 active simultaneously to avoid dilution
- **Submit events 4–5 business days before the start date** to clear App Store review

### Cultural Positioning

- **Thai Buddhist calendar alignment is Mordoo's unique advantage.** No Western astrology app competes on Vesak Day or Asanha Bucha. Lean into this.
- Always provide bilingual copy (Thai primary, English secondary) for every event
- Thai holiday events should reference specific cultural practices (e.g., Loy Krathong wishes, Songkran cleansing) rather than being generic

### ASO Keyword Benefit

In-App Events are indexed by the App Store. Use event names and descriptions to cover keywords you may not rank for organically:

| Target Keywords (EN) | Target Keywords (TH) |
|---|---|
| astrology, horoscope, fortune | ดูดวง, หมอดู, ดวงชะตา |
| Siam Si, fortune sticks | เสียมซี, ใบเสี่ยงทาย |
| daily reading, energy, Prana | ดูดวงรายวัน, พลังงาน, พลังชีวิต |
| Buddhist, Vesak, dharma | วิสาขบูชา, อาสาฬหบูชา, ธรรมะ |
| love compatibility, Valentine | ดูดวงความรัก, วาเลนไทน์ |
| Thai New Year, Songkran | ปีใหม่ไทย, สงกรานต์ |
| lunar eclipse, solstice | จันทรุปราคา, ครีษมายัน |

### Re-engagement Mechanics

- In-App Events trigger **automatic push notifications** to users who have downloaded but are inactive — this is free re-engagement
- Events appear on the **Today tab** and in **Search results** — giving visibility beyond your own product page
- Pair each event with an in-app banner or modal so users who open the app during the event period see relevant content
